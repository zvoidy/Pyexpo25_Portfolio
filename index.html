About PyExpo 25

PYEXPO, an exclusive hackathon event conducted at KGISL Institute of Technology. Organized by the IPS Tech Community , this event provides first-year students with an exciting opportunity to explore the world of hackathons ‚Äì an experience that many have never encountered before. The IPS Tech Community is committed to empowering students by fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical growth.

Purpose of PyExpo 25
The primary goal of PyExpo 25 is to introduce first-year students to the collaborative, problem-solving nature of hackathons. It offers them a platform to nurture their technical skills, promote innovative thinking, and encourage teamwork. By participating in PyExpo 25, students gain hands-on experience in real-world problem-solving while being guided and mentored by seniors and experienced faculty members.

  • IDEA EVALUATION

    Experience the exciting journey of PyExpo'25 as we guide participants from preparation and idea development to the grand finale, showcasing innovation and talent at every step.
  • IDEA2EXPO
    Date : Feb 17-18, 2025
    Faculty mentors evaluate the practical feasibility and trends of ideas. The top 70+ teams proceed to the next stage.
  • HACKSHOP2EXPO
    Date : Feb 19-20 & 24-27, 2025
    Days of intensive workshops for selected teams to refine their ideas and enhance their skills.
  • HACKATHON2EXPO
    Date : Feb 21-22, 2025
    A 30-hour hackathon where teams develop and execute their ideas in a competitive environment.
  • PITCH2EXPO

FAQ's

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open to all students from the first year of every department. Anyone interested in learning and innovating with Python is welcome.

01

Can I form my team with students from other departments?

Of course, interdisciplinary teams are allowed. However, the team will be classified under the team leader's department.

02

Can my friends from other college can apply for the event?

Unfortunately, no. This is an intra-college event.

03

What if I don‚Äôt know how to code?

No problem! Enthusiasm and the willingness to learn are far more important than your current skill level.

04

What are the prizes to be won?

Exciting prizes will be announced soon! Stay tuned for updates!

05

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! You can form teams of 4-6 members, with a mix of design and development skills.
